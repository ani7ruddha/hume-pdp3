30-Day Plan

30-Day Meal Plan — Inspired by @fit.khurana

Vanshika Khurana's signature style: high-protein Indian meals, every meal with macros, homemade low-fat paneer, tofu/soya combos, stevia & double-toned milk swaps, minimal ghee, kasuri methi finishes. ~25-30g protein per meal target.

Inspired by her public reels (paneer steak, low-fat paneer curry, protein theplas, paneer toast, mango shake). Not affiliated with her — adapt portions to your goals.

The fit.khurana rulebook (so this plan actually works)

  • Daily target: 1700-1900 kcal • 100-120g protein • 3-4 L water.
  • Homemade low-fat paneer: her trademark. Boil 1L double-toned milk, add 2 tbsp vinegar/lemon, strain, press 30 min. Yields ~150-180g paneer, ~25g protein, <200 kcal.
  • Sweetener swap: stevia instead of sugar — everywhere.
  • Milk swap: double-toned/skimmed milk only.
  • Veg protein stack: low-fat paneer + tofu + soya chunks + Greek yogurt + whey — combine 2 in one meal to hit 25g+.
  • Flavor profile: kasuri methi, schezwan, sriracha, garlic, kalonji, mixed herbs, chaat masala.
  • Plate rule: protein 25-30g first, complex carbs 50-80g cooked rice or 1-2 phulkas, fistful veggies, 1 tsp ghee max.
  • Pre-workout: black coffee + 1 banana. Post-workout: 1 scoop whey + water or 200g Greek yogurt.
  • 1 cheat meal/week — stay within Sunday dinner. No cheat day, just a meal.
Macros legend: P = protein (g) • C = carbs (g) • F = fat (g) • kcal = calories.

Week 1 — Build the protein floor

Day 1 Mon ~110g P • ~1750 kcal

Breakfast — Besan-paneer chilla (2) P 28 • C 30 • F 12 • 360 kcal
½ cup besan + water + chopped onion, tomato, coriander, ajwain, salt, ¼ tsp red chilli. Cook on tawa with ½ tsp ghee. Stuff with 60g crumbled low-fat paneer + mint chutney.
Lunch — Low-fat paneer curry + 80g rice P 25 • C 50 • F 10 • 430 kcal her signature
Sauté 1 tsp jeera + 5-6 crushed garlic + 2 green chillies in 1 tsp ghee. Add blended 1 onion + 2 tomatoes. Add ½ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp red chilli, ½ tsp dhaniya, 1 tsp garam masala, pinch cinnamon, ½ tsp stevia, salt. Cook 4-5 min. Add 70-80ml double-toned milk + 1 tsp malai, stir. Add 150g paneer cubes + kasuri methi. Cook 5 min.
Dinner — Paneer steak + sautéed veggies + loaded raita P 30 • C 18 • F 14 • 380 kcal her signature
Steak: marinate 150g low-fat paneer in 1 tbsp hot-sweet sauce + 1 tbsp schezwan + 1 tsp olive oil + 2 crushed garlic + 1 tsp soy + 1 tsp vinegar + ½ tsp red chilli + 1 tsp mixed herbs + salt. Rest 15 min, grill both sides.
Veggies: sauté garlic + chilli flakes, toss bell peppers, zucchini, broccoli, corn, mushrooms, carrot, cherry tomato 5 min. Finish with 1 grated cheese cube + soy.
Raita: 150g Greek yogurt + grated carrot + grated boiled beetroot + 2 tbsp pomegranate + chaat masala.

Day 2 Tue ~115g P • ~1800 kcal

Breakfast — Paneer toast (2) P 25 • C 30 • F 12 • 370 kcal her signature
2 slices multigrain toast spread with mash of 150g low-fat paneer + 1 tsp Greek yogurt + finely chopped onion, tomato, capsicum, coriander, green chilli, black pepper, chaat masala. Toast in air-fryer 5 min OR pan-toast 3 min/side.
Lunch — Protein theplas (2) + Greek yogurt P 26 • C 38 • F 8 • 380 kcal her signature
Boil 25g soya chunks, blend with ½ cup peas + handful methi + 2 tbsp onion + 2 green chillies + 3 garlic + salt + red chilli + amchur + pepper + turmeric. Mix with 1 small cup wheat flour + 1 tsp ghee. Rest 10 min. Roll, sprinkle kalonji + sesame, cook on hot tawa with ½ tsp ghee/side. Serve with 90g Greek yogurt.
Dinner — Egg bhurji (3 eggs, 1 yolk) + 1 phulka P 22 • C 22 • F 12 • 320 kcal
Sauté onion-tomato-green chilli-ginger. Add 3 beaten eggs (drop 2 yolks) + turmeric + garam masala + salt. Scramble. Finish with coriander.

Day 3 Wed ~110g P • ~1750 kcal

Breakfast — Greek yogurt protein bowl P 30 • C 30 • F 8 • 320 kcal
200g Greek yogurt + ½ scoop whey whisked smooth + 1 tbsp chia + 1 tbsp oats + stevia + cinnamon. Top with berries + 4 crushed almonds.
Lunch — Tofu-paneer stir fry + 80g rice P 32 • C 50 • F 12 • 470 kcal her veg combo
Cube 100g tofu + 80g low-fat paneer. Sauté 3 garlic + chilli flakes in 1 tsp olive oil. Add capsicum + broccoli + mushrooms + spring onion. Toss tofu + paneer with 1 tsp soy + 1 tsp schezwan + black pepper + mixed herbs. Serve over 80g boiled rice.
Dinner — Methi-malai chicken (light) + 1 phulka P 30 • C 18 • F 10 • 320 kcal
Marinate 150g chicken breast cubes in hung curd + ginger-garlic + turmeric + red chilli + salt 30 min. Sauté with 1 tsp ghee + onion paste, add tomato puree, garam masala, kasuri methi. Splash 50ml double-toned milk at end. Simmer 12 min.

Day 4 Thu ~108g P • ~1750 kcal

Breakfast — Moong dal-paneer chilla P 26 • C 30 • F 8 • 320 kcal
Soak ½ cup yellow moong dal 4 hr. Blend with ginger + green chilli + salt. Spread on tawa with ½ tsp ghee. Top with 60g crumbled paneer + onion + coriander. Fold.
Lunch — Soya kheema + 2 phulkas + kachumber P 30 • C 45 • F 10 • 420 kcal
Soak 40g soya granules in hot water, squeeze. Sauté with onion-tomato-ginger-garlic-green chilli + turmeric + garam masala + dhaniya. Add splash water. Cook 10 min. Finish with lemon + coriander.
Dinner — Grilled chicken + sautéed veggies + 90g Greek yogurt P 38 • C 14 • F 12 • 380 kcal
Marinate 150g chicken in Greek yogurt + ginger-garlic + tandoori masala + lemon. Grill 4 min/side. Sauté broccoli + bell pepper + mushroom in 1 tsp olive oil with garlic + chilli flakes + soy.

Her core tips (from her reels & threads)

  • Idli-sambar is not a balanced meal — not enough protein. Add chutney + paneer/egg side.
  • Make homemade low-fat paneer from double-toned milk — same protein, ~⅓ the fat of store paneer.
  • Hit 25g+ protein in every meal by stacking 2 sources (paneer + Greek yogurt, tofu + paneer, soya + curd).
  • Replace sugar with stevia. Replace full-fat milk with double-toned/skimmed.
  • Cook in 1 tsp ghee — ghee is fine, deep-frying is not.
  • Schezwan + sriracha + kasuri methi do more flavor work than cream or cheese.
  • 1 cheat meal a week, not a cheat day.
  • Walk 8-10k steps + strength train 4x/week. Diet drives fat loss; lifting drives the shape.
